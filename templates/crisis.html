⚠️ Warning Signs to Watch For

  • 💔 Talking about wanting to die or self-harm
  • 😔 Extreme hopelessness or sadness
  • 😴 Severe sleep changes or isolation
  • 😠 Sudden anger or reckless behaviour
  • 📵 Withdrawing from friends and activities
  • 🍷 Increased substance use

🤝 How to Help a Friend

  • 👂 Listen without judgment
  • 💬 Ask directly: "Are you thinking of hurting yourself?"
  • 📞 Help them call a crisis line
  • 🚫 Don't leave them alone in severe distress
  • 📝 Remove access to harmful means if possible
  • 🏥 Escort them to campus health if needed
